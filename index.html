Privacy Policy

Data Processing
Fincy Intelligence processes your uploaded CSV files entirely within your browser session using Streamlit's in-memory runtime. Your data is never transmitted to, stored on, or logged by any Fincy server.

AI Queries
When you use the AI CFO feature, anonymised financial summary statistics (totals, ratios, KPIs) are sent to the Groq API (groq.com) for inference. Raw row-level data from your CSV is never included in AI queries.

Sign-up Data
Name, email, company and role submitted via the sign-up form are stored securely via Formspree and used only to contact you about Fincy Intelligence. We do not sell or share this data with third parties.

Session Isolation
Each user session is completely isolated. No data uploaded by one user is accessible by any other user. All session data is discarded when you close your browser tab.
