FAQ

Questions finance teams
always ask.

Is my financial data safe? Where does it go?+
Your uploaded CSV files are processed entirely within your browser session. The data is never stored on any Fincy server. When you close your tab, the data is gone. When you use the AI CFO, only anonymised financial summary statistics — not raw row-level data — are sent to the Groq API for inference. Each user session is completely isolated.
What format does my CSV need to be in?+
Any format works. Fincy uses dynamic column mapping — you tell the system which column is your Revenue, COGS, EBITDA etc., and it adapts. You can also download our sample templates (6 ready-made CSVs below) to see exactly what works. If you have an Excel file, export it as CSV from File → Save As → CSV.
I'm not technical. Can I still use Fincy?+
Yes — that's exactly who Fincy is built for. If you can upload a file and type a question, you can use every module. No coding, no formulas, no setup. The AI CFO explains everything in plain English. The module cards have direct links that take you straight into the relevant analysis.
Can my team use this together?+
Right now Fincy is a single-user tool — each session is independent. The Team plan (₹3,999/month, coming Q3 2026) will add shared workspaces, multi-user access, and shareable report links. Join the waitlist via the pricing section to get early access and founding user pricing.
How is this different from Power BI or Excel?+
Power BI and Excel require you to build the analysis yourself — models, formulas, dashboards. Fincy gives you the analysis instantly, plus an AI CFO that tells you what to do with the numbers. It took 6 years of FP&A experience at IBM/Reckitt and Capgemini/Unilever to build the logic inside each module. You get that expertise in 60 seconds.
What if I don't have real financial data to upload?+
Use our sample templates — there are 6 ready-made CSVs covering FMCG P&L, reconciliation (ERP vs Bank), budget vs actuals, cost intelligence, a generic dataset, and a cash flow statement. Download any of them from the Templates section below, upload to the app, and your AI CFO will give you a full analysis instantly.
