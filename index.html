Privacy Policy

Data Processing
Your uploaded CSV files are processed entirely within your browser session. Your data is never stored on any Fincy server.

AI Queries
When you use the AI CFO, anonymised financial summary statistics are sent to the Groq API for inference. Raw row-level data is never included in AI queries.

Sign-up Data
Name and email submitted via the sign-up form are stored via Formspree and used only to contact you about Fincy Intelligence.

Session Isolation
Each user session is completely isolated. All session data is discarded when you close your browser tab.
